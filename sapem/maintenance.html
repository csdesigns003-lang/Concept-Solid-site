SapEM Maintenance

Regular maintenance ensures reliable operation of the SapEM monitoring system.

  • Battery Replacement:
    Replace lithium batteries every 2–3 years depending on usage.
  • Sensor Inspection:
    Inspect pressure tubing and ports for debris or condensation.
  • Wireless Signal Check:
    Verify signal strength from the hub to each sensor node.
  • Firmware Updates:
    Check the downloads page periodically for firmware updates.
