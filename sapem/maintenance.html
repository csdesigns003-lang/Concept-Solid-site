SapEM System Maintenance

Routine inspection and maintenance procedures to ensure long-term reliability of SapEM monitoring systems.

Battery Replacement

SapEM sensor nodes are designed for long battery life. Typical battery replacement intervals range from 2 to 3 years depending on transmission rate and environmental conditions.

  • Use only recommended lithium battery types.
  • Ensure polarity is correct during installation.
  • Verify sensor startup LED flashes after replacement.

Sensor Inspection

Inspect pressure tubing and sensor ports periodically to ensure debris, moisture, or contamination has not entered the sensing path.

  • Check tubing connections for leaks.
  • Inspect pressure port for dirt or blockage.
  • Verify enclosure seals remain intact.

Wireless Communication Check

Periodically confirm that each sensor node maintains stable communication with the SapEM hub.

  • Verify signal strength in the monitoring dashboard.
  • Ensure no new obstructions block RF transmission.
  • Reposition hub or sensors if signal strength drops.

Firmware Updates

Firmware updates may provide improved battery life, enhanced diagnostics, or new monitoring features.

  • Check the downloads page for the latest firmware.
  • Follow update procedures in the system manual.
  • Verify successful update through the dashboard.
